About Us

Taksh Crafted Veg Experience blends royal Indian inspiration with modern culinary craft. We are proudly pure vegetarian and dedicated to natural flavors—no artificial flavor enhancers and no ajinomoto. Our chefs design elevated dishes with seasonal produce, creative plating, and balanced textures.

From intimate dinners to large celebrations, we build memorable moments through design, hospitality, and food artistry.
